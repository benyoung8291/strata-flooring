What Makes Us Different

New floors are just the beginning.

Here's the thing most flooring companies won't tell you: a new floor without a maintenance plan is money with an expiry date. Foot traffic, dirt, and time do their thing — and before you know it, you're back to square one.

We don't just install and disappear. We set up a tailored cleaning and care program for your building's floors. Regular deep cleans, protective treatments, and spot care that keeps your investment looking (and performing) at its best.

It's the difference between floors that last a few years and floors that last a decade.

🔄

Scheduled Deep Cleaning

Regular deep cleans that go beyond the surface. We remove ground-in dirt and restore the finish.

🛡️

Protective Treatments

Sealants and coatings that guard against stains, scuffs, and wear — so your floors fight back.

📋

Custom Care Plans

Every building is different. We build a maintenance schedule around your traffic patterns and floor type.

💰

Budget-Friendly Long Game

Spending a little on maintenance now saves a lot on replacement later. Strata committees love that.
