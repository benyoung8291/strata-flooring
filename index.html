Good questions

Things people ask us

How long does strata flooring replacement take?

Most common area flooring replacements take between 2–5 days depending on the size and scope. We work around building schedules to minimise disruption to residents and tenants. You won't need to shut down the whole building — we stage the work so life goes on.

What flooring is best for strata common areas?

Carpet tiles and vinyl planks are the most popular choices. Carpet tiles are brilliant for lobbies and corridors — they're durable, easy to replace individually, and come in a huge range of styles. Vinyl planks work well in high-traffic and moisture-prone areas. We'll help you figure out what makes sense for your building.

Do you handle body corporate approvals?

We provide detailed proposals with product specifications, samples, and costings that make it straightforward for strata managers and body corporates to approve the work. We know the process and we'll make it as painless as possible.

What does a floor maintenance program include?

Our programs are tailored to your flooring type and include regular deep cleaning, protective treatments, spot repairs, and condition reporting. The goal is simple: extend the life of your floors and keep them looking their best year after year.

Can you match existing flooring in other areas?

Yes — we source from all major Australian flooring suppliers and can match or complement your existing flooring to maintain a consistent look throughout the building. Bring us a sample or a photo and we'll sort it out.
